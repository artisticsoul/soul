Artistic Soul

Someone once said, “A true Artist is not the one who is inspired, but the one who inspires others” and I wanted to be that Artist. I believe there is an Artist in everyone, you just need a little inspiration and a paint brush. The sole purpose of this website is to inspire the Artist in you through my work. If there is one word that defines my work it is “Meraki” which means to do something with soul, creativity or love; leaving a piece of yourself in to what you are doing.

Part of you will remain in the form of art forever.

I want to bring art back into your life. I want art to be part of your life.

La Tomatina

I am an Artist. Art is not what I do; Art is how I live my life.

I started painting when I was 5 years old. When I was in 8th grade science took over arts and I quit painting. I am an Indian and I am good at math, so I ended up becoming an Engineer. I never painted for 10 long years, I thought I lost it but few months back I started painting and it felt like magic. I never took a painting class, nor did I ever follow a technique instead I follow my heart when I paint. So, that’s my painting story in short.

I am an adventurous person and I like skiing, biking, hiking and camping. I like to travel, meet interesting people and try different food.
